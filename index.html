It is a period of civil war. Rebel spaceships, striking from a hidden base, have won their first victory against the evil Galactic Empire.

During the battle, Rebel spies managed to steal secret plans to the Empire’s ultimate weapon, the DEATH STAR, an armored space station with enough power to destroy an entire planet.

Pursued by the Empire’s sinister agents, Princess Leia races home aboard her starship, custodian of the stolen plans that can save her people and restore freedom to the galaxy….

A tribute to Star Wars

Star Wars is an epic space-opera media franchise created by George Lucas. All Star Wars is copyright by Lucasfilm Ltd. LLC and The Wallt Disney Studios. This website is a tribute to the emblematic characters created by George Lucas.

Star Corridors is an idea, created, designed and developed by Acózar.

All corridors panels are designed by Acózar. Death Star panels are designed from originals decals by Frank Diorio (Diorama Workshop). Panels can be downloaded for free to use as backgrounds with action figures dioramas or displays. Non commercial use is allowed.
